# Plain-Language Product Summary

## Source Product Spec
- Path: `examples/product-spec-example.md`
- spec_id: `SPEC-20260521-onboarding-clarity`
- spec_version: `1.0.0`

## What Is Being Built
An onboarding improvement so non-technical users can complete setup with fewer confusing moments.

## Who It Is For
Primary users are first-time non-technical workspace owners.
Secondary users are support agents who answer onboarding questions.

## Why It Matters
Users currently stop during onboarding because they are unsure what information is needed and why.
This causes drop-off and repeated support requests.

## What Is Included
- Clearer onboarding flow intent.
- Ability to cancel onboarding at any step without losing saved progress.
- Risk-review step before high-risk approval actions.

## What Is Not Included
- Full redesign of account settings.
- Building a new analytics platform.

## Success Criteria In Plain Language
- Most users should finish onboarding successfully (target: completion rate above 80%).
- Support questions about onboarding should drop significantly (target: 40% reduction).
- Pilot users should be able to explain what each onboarding step asks for.

## Main Risks
- Simpler wording might accidentally hide legal meaning if wording is not reviewed.
- Partial updates could improve one step but leave confusion in other steps.
- These risks may require legal and support review before downstream planning.
